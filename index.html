Hi! My name is Yash Vardhan Bhobia.

Researcher | Senior year undergrad

Hello there! I'm Yash Vardhan Bhobia, currently pursuing a major in Material Science with specializations in Spintronics, Magnetic Devices, and Micromagnetic Simulations as an senior year undergrad at Indian Institute of Science(IISc). I am a National Talent Search Examination Awardee, Kishore Vigyan Protshan Yojna Fellow, and Inspire DST Fellow. Beyond my current research interests, I'm captivated by Quantum Computing, neuromorphic computing, and related domains. On the extracurricular front, I've served as the convenor of "Debug" (CS-Coding UG Club at IISc), Chief Finance Coordinator at the annual cultural and Sci-tech fest of IISc, Pravega, and engaged in private tutoring for international Olympiads and college admission examinations(IIT-JEE) in India. I'm also a proud volunteer for the NoteBook Drive, providing free education to underprivileged primary school kids. Outside the academic realm, I find joy in bike rides to hill stations, surrounded by nature's beauty. I'm an avid reader of poetry, enjoy sketching portraits, indulge in classic music, and find relaxation in watching anime. Basketball and badminton are my go-to sports, adding a dynamic touch to my interests. Looking ahead, my passion for teaching drives a long-term goal of mentoring and providing free, quality education for young minds. I aspire to contribute to the next technological revolution as we navigate the ever-evolving landscape of our computational world.
